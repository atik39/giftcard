WHY SHOULD YOU
RENT TO OWN?

Renting to Own is one of the best ways to get into your own house. It's also sometimes called Lease With Option to Buy and is a common practice in your state. When you Rent to Own, your deposit may be able to apply towards your down payment, and a portion of your regular rent may also apply towards the downpayment.

This means you can skip costly mortgage insurance while getting to know your neighbourhood. The whole process also builds on your history of savings and payments, and that can your credit rating and your chances of later qualifying for a conventional mortgage.
